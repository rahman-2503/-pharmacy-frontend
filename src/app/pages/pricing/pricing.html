Pricing Frequently Asked Questions

Got questions about subscription renewals or custom integrations? We have answers.

Can I change my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, your payment will be prorated based on the remaining days of your current cycle.

Are there any setup fees?

No, there are no hidden setup fees. All standard onboarding training, data imports, and initial supplier configurations are included in the annual fee.

Do you offer monthly cycles?

To reduce administrative billing overhead and offer the lowest prices possible, our standard plans are billed annually. Custom monthly options are available for Capsule/Injection packages upon request.

Is my database backed up securely?

Absolutely. All databases are securely hosted on AWS with automatic point-in-time recovery and database mirroring, fully compliant with FHIR regulations.
